Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our co-op mailers

We limit each mailer to 8-12 businesses to ensure each participant gets adequate visibility. The exact number depends on the mailer size and distribution area.

The most successful offers provide clear value and urgency. Examples include:

  • "20% off your first visit"
  • "Buy one, get one free"
  • "$25 value for just $10"
  • "Free consultation with coupon"

We'll help you craft an offer that drives results for your specific business.

While we handle all design work to maintain consistency, we welcome your input! You can provide your logo, brand colors, and any specific imagery you'd like included. Our designers will create a professional layout that matches the overall mailer design while highlighting your business.

Each mailer reaches between 5,000-10,000 targeted local households, depending on your business location and the participating businesses. We use sophisticated demographic targeting to reach the most likely customers for all participating businesses.

We analyze the locations of all participating businesses and select zip codes that provide the best coverage for everyone. Our goal is to reach potential customers within a reasonable distance (typically 3-5 miles) from each business.

We typically schedule mailings every 6-8 weeks. Once we have enough businesses signed up for a particular area, we'll confirm the mailing date (usually 3-4 weeks from signup to allow for design and printing). You'll receive advance notice so you can prepare your staff.
